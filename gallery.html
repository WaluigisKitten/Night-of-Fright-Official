Night of Fright Gallery

Pics of all our spooky attractions

Prepare for a pulse-pounding journey through the twisted woods of "Terror Trail." This spine-chilling haunted experience delivers a brief yet unforgettable encounter with the supernatural. You'll navigate through the inky darkness, where lurking horrors and spectral surprises await at every corner. In just a few short minutes, you'll feel your heart race and your nerves tingle as you confront your deepest fears. "Terror Trail" offers a condensed dose of terror that's perfect for thrill-seekers on the go. Come and experience an adrenaline-fueled adventure that will leave you with a haunting memory to ponder long after you've escaped its clutches.

Step into a world of Halloween enchantment at our "Spooky Bites Galore" food court! With ghoulish delights and spine-tingling treats, this is the ultimate destination for foodies and Halloween enthusiasts alike.

Our food court is transformed into a Halloween wonderland, complete with eerie decorations, jack-o'-lanterns, and a macabre ambiance that sets the perfect stage for your dining pleasure. Savor the flavors of the season with a diverse array of Halloween-themed dishes and drinks. From pumpkin-spiced everything to creatively crafted "monster" burgers, we've conjured up a menu that's bound to satisfy your cravings.

Stroll along the creaky wooden boardwalk, adorned with ghostly lanterns and mysterious shipwreck artifacts. Delight in the hauntingly captivating sights and sounds of the season, with costumed pier staff and spooky decor around every corner. Trick-or-treaters of all ages will find thrills, chills, and excitement at every turn.

Enter the realm of your darkest nightmares at "Nightmare Manor," where terror and thrills await around every corner. This haunted house experience is not for the faint of heart, as it plunges you into a world of chilling suspense and spine-tingling surprises.

Dare to venture through the labyrinthine halls, where sinister specters, malevolent creatures, and restless spirits come to life. As you wind your way through dimly lit corridors and eerie chambers, you'll be immersed in a chilling narrative that will keep you on the edge of your seat.
